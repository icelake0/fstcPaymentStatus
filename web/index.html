Sam Cloud CGPA Calculator

This App was developed by Kayode Samuel Babatunde (1406172077) under the supervision of Mr. Oluwole Arajo at the Department of Computer Science, Lagos State Polytechnic, Ikorodu, Lagos. The App is a cloud CGPA calculator based on the new 4.0 scale grading system of lagos state polytechnic. Registered students can login using any Android device that have the app. They can go on to view and update their grades and they can save and calculate their CGPA.
